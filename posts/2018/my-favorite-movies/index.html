My Favorite Hollywood Movies

I am a big fan of Quentin Tarantino and I love all of his movies. But the following movies are top notch in my opinion and I never get bored of watching them.

  • Pulp Fiction
  • Django Unchained
  • Kill Bill: Volume 1
  • Kill Bill: Volume 2
  • Inglourious Basterds
  • The Hateful Eight

Next, I am huge fan of Clint Eastwood and I love his movies too. Following are my favorite movies acted in or directed by Clint Eastwood.

  • The Good, the Bad and the Ugly
  • Million Dollar Baby
  • Gran Torino
  • The Bridges of Madison County

The following movies are my favorite movies I like to watch over and over again.

  • It’s A Wonderful Life
  • The Shawshank Redemption
  • Schindler’s List
  • Forrest Gump
  • The Pursuit of Happyness
  • Life is Beautiful
  • 12 Angry Men
  • Fight Club
  • God Father
  • Gone Girl
